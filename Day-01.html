Opportunity Cost & Scarcity

The idea under everything: the cost of a choice is the best thing you gave up.

⏸ Before you listen - guess first

You have one free evening. You could push your side-project forward, or rest. You choose to rest. Before reading on — what did resting actually cost you?

Take 20 seconds and answer in your head, then press play.

🎧 Listen ~5 min

Play the episode, then work through the question below.

  • Opportunity cost - the value of the single next-best thing you gave up to make a choice.
  • Not the money price - it is the lost alternative, not the cash you handed over.

It is zero only when you could genuinely do both at once. If the two overlap in time, the choice always has a cost.
