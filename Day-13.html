Formula sheet (given on the exam):
GDP
Nominal GDP = Σ (this-year price × qty)
Real GDP = Σ (base-year price × this-year qty)
Real GDP per capita = Real GDP / population
Prices
CPI = (basket this year / basket base year) × 100
Inflation = (CPI2 − CPI1) / CPI1 × 100
Elasticity
Elasticity = %ΔQ / %ΔP
%ΔQ = elasticity × %ΔP
Jobs
Labour force = employed + unemployed
Participation = LF / working-age × 100
Unemployment = unemployed / LF × 100
Costs
TC = FC + VC · MC = ΔTC
ATC = TC/Q · AVC = VC/Q
Profit = TR − TC = P·Q − TC
Firm decisions
Shutdown (short run): produce if P ≥ AVC
Exit (long run): stay if P ≥ ATC
Market: trade the short side
